Introduction

The Sarawak Museum Department is the government agency responsible for the management and development of museums in the state of Sarawak, Malaysia. The department oversees several museums, including the Sarawak Museum, one of the oldest museums in Southeast Asia, and the Borneo Cultures Museum, which is Malaysia's largest museum and the second largest in Southeast Asia.

Visit our website at museum.sarawak.gov.my for the latest information.

How to get the service?

Important Notes

  • Ticket will be sent to the given email address as a PDF, which will be used at the museum entrance.
  • Ticket is valid for one-time redemption only and is non-transferable, non-exchangeable, and non-refundable.
  • Ticket is valid and must be redeemed within 90 days from the date of purchase.
  • Borneo Cultures Museum Operating Hours:
    • Monday - Friday (9.00am - 4.45pm)
    • Saturday, Sunday, Public Holidays (9.30am - 4.30pm)
  • Last admission to the museum is at 4.00pm.
  • Closed on public holidays:
    • Eid al-Fitr (First day)
    • Chinese New Year (First day)
    • Labour Day
    • Gawai Day (First day)
    • Sarawak Governor's Birthday
    • Independence Day
    • Christmas Day
  • Group ticket (more than 10 pax), Registered Person With Disabilities, and Annual Pass purchases are advisable to be made at the museum ticketing counter to benefit from the discount.
